We facilitate the researchers and groups of individuals working in the field of Image Dehazing by providing Quantitative Analysis of their datasets by understanding results from Reside Dataset.

Need of Visibility Enhancer

Visibility is a crucial issue faced by outdoor vision-based systems. Mediocre weather reduces the visibility affecting performance of outdoor image processing-based systems. Atmospheric particles scatter and absorb light that is reflected from the surface of an object, resulting in the incorrect magnitude of the irradiance. Hence, the images and videos fade in bad weather. 

learn more

What's New?

Analyze your dataset using our new model designed to estimate accurate parameters of atmospheric scattering model. 
Also analyze the performance and Quantitative Analysis of state of art dehazing methods.

Our Aim

Our aim is to provide more visually pleasing images and scene radiance to the users. Haze removal can also provide depth information and benefit to many vision algorithms and advanced images. For this, we are also  developing  a mobile application which takes an input hazy image from the user and converts it into a haze - free image.
